About me

Where I'm from

I was born in Kyiv and raised there. From an early age, I was interested in technology, the Internet, and programming. After an institute, I switched several jobs, doing tech support for PC and Mac, some content management, and so on. At some point, I ended up managing a website for the magazine Computer Review, first as a content manager, then as an administrator, and finally as a web developer.

What I'm doing

At this point, I'm finishing building a new content management platform for Computer Review. For several years I also work as a freelancer and build web services and sites for clients. I'm a big fan of online education and self-improvement, so I actively learn about different aspects of programming, project management, and web technologies.

My plans

To be better! I want to be the best developer I can possibly be. To achieve this I want to work with experienced devs, continue to learn all aspects of front- and back-end development, as well as create reliable, fast, and scalable services.
